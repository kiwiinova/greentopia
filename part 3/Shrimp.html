Product Description

Having a firm and crunchy texture, northern shrimp, better known as Matane shrimp, have a fine and delicate taste. ... Low in fat, they are notably a source of protein, Omega-3, Omega-6 and vitamin B12. Its consumption is therefore excellent for heart health and for strengthening the immune system. However, the consumption of Nordic shrimp should be done in moderation since they are also high in cholesterol.
